Biography

I am a PhD student at The University of Queensland. My research focuses on AI infrastructure security, cloud-native systems, Kubernetes security, software security, and program analysis.

Before starting my PhD, I worked for more than ten years in cloud computing, distributed systems, Kubernetes-based platforms, AI infrastructure, and enterprise software engineering. My industrial experience has shaped my research interest in the security, reliability, and correctness of real-world AI and cloud-native systems.

My current research explores security problems in modern AI infrastructure, especially Kubernetes-based AI platforms, admission control, RBAC, GPU resource management, container orchestration, and LLM-driven vulnerability discovery.
